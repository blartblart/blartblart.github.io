Independence Study Writing Prompts -- 8/28/2023

Writing prompts for independent study:

  1. Talk about a favorite gift you've received.
  2. Write about a piece of advice you'll never forget.
  3. Write about a time you thought you lost everything.
  4. Write about your favorite vacation.
  5. Write about a favorite time out to dinner.
  6. Write about a time when you were shocked and couldn't speak.
